you and your CHIHUAHUA PUPPY
in a nutshell

The world’s smallest pure breed dog

Small and immeasurably cute, Chihuahuas are brilliant for apartment living. They adore their humans, make hugely affectionate companion dogs, and are ever so easy to scoop up and carry.

But with this they can easily become spoilt. Sadly, many owners don’t give them the same training they would give a bigger dog, while in fact Chihuahuas need just as much guidance – if not more – to grow up happy and well-adjusted in a world of people rules.

There’s a huge amount you can do to be an excellent ‘parent’ to your Chihuahua puppy, and you can find it here.
