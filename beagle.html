you and your BEAGLE PUPPY in a nutshell

The essential owners' guide to perfect puppy parenting - with easy-to-follow steps on how to choose and care for your new arrival

Part of the Series: You and Your Puppy in a Nutshell

The perfect handbook for Beagle puppy parents-to-be! A compact summary of the latest findings in puppy care and behaviour, this book walks you step by step through the process - from deciding whether a Beagle is right for you, to the first crucial days, weeks and months.

This book offers amusing dos and don'ts of puppy care that will keep you smiling through the poops, night-time whimpering and shredded personal items. And its easy-to-read, easy-to-follow advice will help you bring up a well-behaved dog, and get your special relationship off to a wonder-filled start.

About Beagle puppies

Yes, Beagles are lovely looking dogs. They’re good natured and make wonderful family companions.

  • Colourful velvety coats, large floppy ears and adoring eyes.
  • Playful, affectionate and sensitive to your feelings.
  • Arguably the perfect size, easy to care for and a generally healthy breed.

But these happy little hounds will also bring you buckets of love and laughter, because they’re endearingly independent – curious to the core, easily led astray by their exceptional noses and ingenious problem solvers.
