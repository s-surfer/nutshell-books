you and your COCKAPOO PUPPY in a nutshell

The essential owners' guide to perfect puppy parenting - with easy-to-follow steps on how to choose and care for your new arrival

Part of the Series: You and Your Puppy in a Nutshell

The perfect handbook for Cockapoo puppy parents-to-be! A compact summary of the latest findings in puppy care and behaviour, this book walks you step by step through the process - from deciding whether a Cockapoo is right for you, to the first crucial days, weeks and months.

This book offers amusing dos and don'ts of puppy care that will keep you smiling through the poops, night-time whimpering and shredded personal items. And its easy-to-read, easy-to-follow advice will help you bring up a well-behaved dog, and get your special relationship off to a wonder-filled start.

About Cockapoo puppies

Teddy-bear appeal, rubber-ball bounce and overwhelming affection for one and all. It’s no wonder these dogs have become so popular.

They’re also commendably adaptable, typically low-shedding and make wonderful companions. A Cockapoo will leave you loopy with love!

But don’t be fooled into thinking these dogs will lounge around. With Cocker Spaniel and Poodle in their make-up they can be extremely bouncy, and need a great deal of mental and physical exercise.

NOTE: Cockapoos want to be with their humans all the time. Don’t get one if you’re going to be out at work all day.
