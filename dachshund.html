you and your DACHSHUND PUPPY in a nutshell

The essential owners' guide to perfect puppy parenting - with easy-to-follow steps on how to choose and care for your new arrival

Part of the Series: You and Your Puppy in a Nutshell

The perfect handbook for Dachshund puppy parents-to-be! A compact summary of the latest findings in puppy care and behaviour, this book walks you step by step through the process - from deciding whether a Dachshund is right for you, to the first crucial days, weeks and months.

This book offers amusing dos and don'ts of puppy care that will keep you smiling through the poops, night-time whimpering and shredded personal items. And its easy-to-read, easy-to-follow advice will help you bring up a well-behaved dog, and get your special relationship off to a wonder-filled start.

About Dachshund puppies

Dachs are a constant source of amusement. You’d be hard pressed to find any breed as comical, characterful and, well … utterly fascinating.

Bright as buttons, but selectively obedient, they use their heart-melting gorgeousness to get away with all manner of mischief.

Be warned, they will dig their small sausage selves into the most unreachable nooks and crannies of your house. And your heart.
