you and your LABRADOR PUPPY in a nutshell

The essential owners' guide to perfect puppy parenting - with easy-to-follow steps on how to choose and care for your new arrival

Part of the Series: You and Your Puppy in a Nutshell

The perfect handbook for Labrador puppy parents-to-be! A compact summary of the latest findings in puppy care and behaviour, this book walks you step by step through the process - from deciding whether a Labrador is right for you, to the first crucial days, weeks and months.

This book offers amusing dos and don'ts of puppy care that will keep you smiling through the poops, night-time whimpering and shredded personal items. And its easy-to-read, easy-to-follow advice will help you bring up a well-behaved dog, and get your special relationship off to a wonder-filled start.

About Labrador puppies

This is the world’s favourite breed, and for very good reason. Labradors get top marks in every department. They are loving and good-natured; sociable and playful; cooperative and eager to please; tolerant and biddable; open and trusting…. Be ready for your puppy to bowl you right over with its big love and, yes, its boisterous bounding too.

Don’t choose a Lab if you’re after a guard dog – they’re far too affectionate. They also have insatiable appetites but, on the bright side, never again will you have to pick food up off the floor.

NOTE:

To avoid joint or sight problems in later life, choose a responsibly bred puppy with the relevant health clearance certificates.
