you and your CAVALIER KING CHARLES SPANIEL PUPPY in a nutshell

The essential owners' guide to perfect puppy parenting - with easy-to-follow steps on how to choose and care for your new arrival

Part of the Series: You and Your Puppy in a Nutshell

The perfect handbook for Cavalier King Charles puppy parents-to-be! A compact summary of the latest findings in puppy care and behaviour, this book walks you step by step through the process - from deciding whether a Cavalier King Charles is right for you, to the first crucial days, weeks and months.

This book offers amusing dos and don'ts of puppy care that will keep you smiling through the poops, night-time whimpering and shredded personal items. And its easy-to-read, easy-to-follow advice will help you bring up a well-behaved dog, and get your special relationship off to a wonder-filled start.

About Cavalier King Charles puppies

This is the sweetest, gentlest and most empathetic of breeds. Responsibly-bred and well-raised, Cavalier King Charles Spaniels don’t have a bad bone in their bodies.

They’re extremely tactile and want little more than to cuddle and be cuddled. In fact, they will drape their long-eared sliky-soft selves over you at every opportunity.

But be warned, they do love to be the centre of attention and they can be easliy spoilt.

Choosing your Cavvie puppy carefully is hugely important if you want to increase its chances of a long, healthy and happy life.
