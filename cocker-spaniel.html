you and your COCKER SPANIEL PUPPY in a nutshell

The essential owners' guide to perfect puppy parenting - with easy-to-follow steps on how to choose and care for your new arrival

Part of the Series: You and Your Puppy in a Nutshell

The perfect handbook for Cocker Spaniel puppy parents-to-be! A compact summary of the latest findings in puppy care and behaviour, this book walks you step by step through the process - from deciding whether a Cocker Spaniel is right for you, to the first crucial days, weeks and months.

This book offers amusing dos and don'ts of puppy care that will keep you smiling through the poops, night-time whimpering and shredded personal items. And its easy-to-read, easy-to-follow advice will help you bring up a well-behaved dog, and get your special relationship off to a wonder-filled start.

About Cocker Spaniel puppies

Cocker Spaniels are full of the joys of living. With their ever-wagging tails, they’re like a big dose of sunshine. And their engaging eyes, soft dangly ears and silky-smooth fur make them near impossible to resist.

Friendly, eager to please, and a manageable size, these dogs make excellent family pets.

They’re intelligent, with a delightful hint of madness, and can be highly energetic. The working Cockers especially love a good run around outdoors, investigating the surroundings with their excellent noses.

Note: Cockers need company, so don’t choose one if you’re going to be out often and for long stretches.
