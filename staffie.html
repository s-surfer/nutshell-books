you and your STAFFIE PUPPY in a nutshell

The essential owners' guide to perfect puppy parenting - with easy-to-follow steps on how to choose and care for your new arrival

Part of the Series: You and Your Puppy in a Nutshell

The perfect handbook for Staffie puppy parents-to-be! A compact summary of the latest findings in puppy care and behaviour, this book walks you step by step through the process - from deciding whether a Staffie is right for you, to the first crucial days, weeks and months.

This book offers amusing dos and don'ts of puppy care that will keep you smiling through the poops, night-time whimpering and shredded personal items. And its easy-to-read, easy-to-follow advice will help you bring up a well-behaved dog, and get your special relationship off to a wonder-filled start.

About Staffie puppies

Your Staffie will beam up at you with its wide-mouthed smile – a typical trait and telltale hint of the huge love these dogs have for you, and the whole human family.

With their stocky builds, Staffies can look intimidating, but in fact they want nothing more than to give you their love, attention, friendship and loyalty. They give 100% and deserve nothing short of the very best in return.

NOTES:

  • If you are willing to take on an adolescent or older dog, there are plenty of beautiful and deserving Staffies in our rehoming centres.
  • In this book, ’Staffie’ refers to the Staffordshire Bull Terrier, but the care advice can also be applied to the ‘AmStaff’ or American Staffordshire Terrier.
