in a nutshell

Whether you’re new to owning a dog, simply out of practice, or choosing a breed you have little experience with, here is all the information you will need (and none of the padding you don’t) to raise a happy, healthy and well-behaved companion.

Each book is a summary of the most up-to-date findings in puppy care, presented in a format that is clear, concise and easy to read, so you can spend more time with your puppy and less time with your nose in the pages.

All Puppies Guide

You and Your Puppy in a Nutshell covers pure breeds, cross breeds and mixed breeds alike. This is the best choice for you if you are:

  • considering dog ownership in principle
  • or weighing up what type of dog would be the best fit for your household and lifestyle.
