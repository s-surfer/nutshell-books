you and your GERMAN SHEPHERD PUPPY in a nutshell

The essential owners' guide to perfect puppy parenting - with easy-to-follow steps on how to choose and care for your new arrival

Part of the Series: You and Your Puppy in a Nutshell

The perfect handbook for German Shepherd puppy parents-to-be! A compact summary of the latest findings in puppy care and behaviour, this book walks you step by step through the process - from deciding whether a German Shepherd is right for you, to the first crucial days, weeks and months.

This book offers amusing dos and don'ts of puppy care that will keep you smiling through the poops, night-time whimpering and shredded personal items. And its easy-to-read, easy-to-follow advice will help you bring up a well-behaved dog, and get your special relationship off to a wonder-filled start.

About German Shepherd puppies

No other breed has helped so many people in so many ways. German Shepherds are heroes in life, just as they are in film and fiction.

Like all true heroes, they are self-assured without being overbearing; and strong and fearless without being hostile or aggressive.

Their instincts are remarkable. Your German Shepherd will know your true motives, how you’re feeling, what you’re thinking and probably what you’ve got planned for next week.

And s/he will stand by you with unfailing love, care and protection.
