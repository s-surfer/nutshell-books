you and your PUPPY in a nutshell

The essential owners' guide to perfect puppy parenting - with easy-to-follow steps on how to choose and care for your new arrival

Part of the Series: You and Your Puppy in a Nutshell

A compact summary of the latest findings in puppy care and behaviour, this guide will help you be the best puppy parent you can possibly be, and get your special relationship off to a wonder-filled start.

With easy-to-read, easy-to-follow advice, it walks you step by step from deciding whether a puppy is right for you, to the daunting first days and weeks, and through the first crucial months. Its amusing DOs and DON'Ts of puppy care will keep you smiling through the poops, night-time whimpers and shredded items of personal value.

About the book

You and Your Puppy in a Nutshell covers pure breeds, cross breeds and mixed breeds alike. This is the best choice for you if you are:

  • considering dog ownership in principle,
  • or weighing up what type of dog would be a good fit for your household and lifestyle.

It is a compact summary of the latest findings in puppy care and behaviour that guides you step by step through the process – from choosing your puppy and preparing for the homecoming, to those first exciting and sometimes overwhelming days, and through the crucial early weeks and months when your puppy is growing and learning at its fastest.
