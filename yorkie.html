you and your YORKIE PUPPY in a nutshell

The essential owners' guide to perfect puppy parenting - with easy-to-follow steps on how to choose and care for your new arrival

Part of the Series: You and Your Puppy in a Nutshell

The perfect handbook for Yorkie puppy parents-to-be! A compact summary of the latest findings in puppy care and behaviour, this book walks you step by step through the process - from deciding whether a Yorkie is right for you, to the first crucial days, weeks and months.

This book offers amusing dos and don'ts of puppy care that will keep you smiling through the poops, night-time whimpering and shredded personal items. And its easy-to-read, easy-to-follow advice will help you bring up a well-behaved dog, and get your special relationship off to a wonder-filled start.

About Yorkie puppies

Utterly precious and bursting with character, these toy-terriers will win your hearts with their silky-smooth cuddles and bright-eyed devotion.

Yorkies are incredibly sweet, but you’ll need an excellent sense of humour because they make astoundingly spirited companions. Part lapdog, part feisty terrier, they can be glitzy and glamourous one minute, and little ruffians the next.

Well-raised and well-treated, a Yorkshire Terrier will bring buckets of love, happiness and, yes, attitude to your home.
