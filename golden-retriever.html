you and your GOLDEN RETRIEVER PUPPY in a nutshell

The essential owners' guide to perfect puppy parenting - with easy-to-follow steps on how to choose and care for your new arrival

Part of the Series: You and Your Puppy in a Nutshell

The perfect handbook for Golden Retriever puppy parents-to-be! A compact summary of the latest findings in puppy care and behaviour, this book walks you step by step through the process - from deciding whether a Golden Retriever is right for you, to the first crucial days, weeks and months.

This book offers amusing dos and don'ts of puppy care that will keep you smiling through the poops, night-time whimpering and shredded personal items. And its easy-to-read, easy-to-follow advice will help you bring up a well-behaved dog, and get your special relationship off to a wonder-filled start.

About Golden Retriever puppies

Big, soft and beautiful from the tips of their shiny noses to the tips of their sweeping tails. And they’re every bit as gorgeous on the inside too.

Golden Retrievers will love you unconditionally, trust you completely, and forgive you wholeheartedly, and repeatedly. When it comes to true love, they’re a tough act to follow.

They may be shedders – and there’s no denying that they love a bit of water (and mud too) – but if you’ve got the space and energy for a big dog, and you’re after a loving, intelligent and devoted companion, this is an excellent choice.
